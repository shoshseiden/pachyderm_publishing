Publishing Pricing

Rates

  • * 300 pages and under - $450.00 - book cover, minor editing and upload
  •                                        $250.00 - no book cover (author has their own), minor editing and upload
  • * Over 300-500 pages - $650.00 - book cover minor editing and upload

  • *** Once the final draft and artwork are given to the author for their approval, there will only be two revisions done (author will be given ample time to go over the final draft and cover before final upload and release date.)
